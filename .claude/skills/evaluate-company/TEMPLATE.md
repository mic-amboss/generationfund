## Bull thesis (in the language of the four criteria)

### 1. Great Business

Why this is a great business. Specific moats, ROIC figures, capital intensity, cash conversion, pricing power, balance-sheet quality. Cite the deep dive.

### 2. Principled, idiosyncratic owner-operator(s)

Why the operator(s) clear the bar. Specific decisions, capital allocation evidence, communication style, partnership posture. Cite the deep dive.

### 3. Long runway for profitable compounding

Why the runway supports 10+ years of high-ROIC reinvestment. TAM, share dynamics, reinvestment engine, structural tailwind, optionality. Cite the deep dive.

Why the current price clears the 15%+ forward IRR hurdle. Base / bull / bear scenarios with shown work. Note the assumptions that drive each.
